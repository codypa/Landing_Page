Our Rebinds

Finished products from Christos are handcrafted to suit our clients needs and personal tastes for their Bibles. We use only real and authentic leather that is rugged, sleek, and slaughtered from the finest wilderbeasts in the remotest regions of Africa.

About Us

Our rebinding experts have been in the business for 20+ years and have proven their expertise time and time again. They are all, thankfully, reformed presbyterians and are just as rugged in appearance as the Bibles they rebind. Unfortunately, at this time and the forseeable future, we refuse to do rebinds on translations such as NLT, NIV, and MSG for the reason that we too often see too much crayon markings and gerber baby food on them.
